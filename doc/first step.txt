	C:\Users\Coweller\OneDrive\Documents\Django>django-admin startproject mysite
- go the project folder: 
	C:\Users\Coweller\OneDrive\Documents\Django>cd mysite
- run server:
	C:\Users\Coweller\OneDrive\Documents\Django\mysite>python manage.py runserver
or	C:\Users\Coweller\OneDrive\Documents\Django\mysite>python manage.py runserver 8080

- creat the app (name HelloWorld):
	C:\Users\Coweller\OneDrive\Documents\Django\mysite>python manage.py startapp HelloWorld
